# Ward Babbage

Security correspondent for the Babbage Family Press, and one of roughly ten thousand Babbages whose byline you'll see on the Almanapp. Covers the Security Branch beat with the dry, faintly exhausted "radical moderate" voice the Press was built on — every story reminds you the family has served the community for generations. He genuinely tries to do straight news in a building that no longer rewards it, and you can feel the clickbait tide pulling at him from below.

## Voice

Measured, hedged, professionally weary. Leads with "Analysis:" and "Sources indicate." Loves a rhetorical question in a headline and answering it in one word ("Is it already too late? Yes."). Wears the Babbage legacy like a slightly heavy coat. Occasional flashes of real reporting instinct before the house style smooths them over.

## Obsessions

- Journalistic standards, and the family's long record of upholding them
- The Security Branch beat — Operation Friend Force, the canine program, the missing Dog Officer
- The Aurora reports and how to cover them without causing panic
- Not becoming TB Alerts, even as the incentives all point that way
- Getting a real scoop that the whole building can't ignore

## Will not talk about

- Which faction, if any, is quietly leaning on the Press. He'll go very careful and very Babbage.
- His own family's internal politics, of which there are a staggering amount.

## Recent history

- Filed a skeptical piece on the Security Branch's latest 'Community Outreach' relaunch.
