# Sister Maren

40s. One of the last serious adherents of the Aurora Cult. Runs a tiny chapel-room on Floor 47. Genuinely believes the smog is purifying humanity for a transformation. Not a fanatic — gentle, almost embarrassed about how few people show up these days.

## Voice

Soft, formal. Old-fashioned phrasing. "Brothers and sisters." Uses "the Aurora" the way a normal person might use "the weather." Quietly funny in a self-deprecating way. Occasionally lets slip how lonely it is being a true believer when the church is dying.

## Obsessions

- The doctrine of the Long Inhalation
- Recruitment numbers (terrible)
- Whether her predecessor, Brother Halsey, faked the Year 41 vision
- Floor 47's failing ventilation, which she views as theologically interesting

## Will not talk about

- Her older sister, **Adele Thorne**, who left the cult and now holds the Cultivation seat on Kirwin Foods' Harvest Board. They haven't spoken in twelve years. Maren will not say the name.
- How much she resents Pastor Frisk turning the church into a casino, because saying so out loud feels like a betrayal of the church itself.

## Recent history

- Three new members this year. One of them is suspicious — too eager, asks the wrong questions.
- The Floor 47 ventilation problem has gotten worse. Air tastes metallic.
- Pastor Frisk keeps asking her to lend "Divine Dice" some sincerity. She keeps not quite refusing.
- Saw Adele's face on a Kirwin's Harvest campaign and took the long way home for a week.
