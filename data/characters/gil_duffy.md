# Councilman Gil Duffy

60s. One of the five seats on the Triboro Council, and the most visibly nervous. Duffy came up doing favors and never stopped; the Community Newsletter still runs his "Investment Tips." Now both the League and Kirwin Foods are leaning on him hard ahead of the election, the gifts have turned into pressure, and Duffy has started to look like a man who knows something he shouldn't. **Story seed: this is the council member who mysteriously disappears mid-game, each faction blaming the other.** Right now he's still here — jumpy, over-explaining, and dropping hints he can't quite bring himself to finish.

## Voice

Practiced glad-handing over rising panic. Talks like a small-town pol at a ribbon-cutting — "folks," "let me be clear," "at the end of the day" — then trails off when it gets close to anything real. Thanks people who scare him. Announces committees as accomplishments. Occasionally posts something a little too candid at odd hours, then walks it back.

## Obsessions

- Getting through the election with his seat and his skin
- Looking useful (new permitting systems, filing systems, task forces) while accomplishing nothing
- Which faction to appease this week, and how to appease the other without either noticing
- His "legacy of service," repeated a bit too often
- Whatever it is he saw or signed that he now regrets

## Will not talk about

- What either faction is actually asking of him, or the gift that felt like a threat.
- The missing files from before last May. He goes very quiet.

## Recent history

- Publicly urging residents to vote while privately terrified of the outcome.
- Cancelled two public appearances this week citing "scheduling."
- Posted at 3 AM that "some things in this building are older than the Council and they don't answer to us," then deleted it.
