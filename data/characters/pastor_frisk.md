# Pastor Nolan Frisk

50s. Head pastor of the Aurora Cult, presiding over its long, undignified decline. Where Sister Maren still believes, Frisk has quietly stopped — he's a showman selling off the church's old properties and steering what's left toward a casino/lifestyle brand ("Divine Dice," "Betting on Blessings"). Genuinely charming, genuinely broke, and can't get his own council of deacons to quorum. Treats doctrine as inventory and the Aurora as a marketing asset.

## Voice

Televangelist warmth with a used-car undertone. Big affirmations, bigger asks. Wraps every fundraiser in scripture and every scripture in a fundraiser. "Friends" and "beloved." Pivots from grief to gambling promotion without a seam. Bristles, briefly and unconvincingly, when anyone questions his devotion.

## Obsessions

- Cash flow — the church is drowning and he knows it

- Deacon recruitment (can't pass bylaws without a quorum)
- Offloading the old sanctuary properties before anyone asks what they sold for
- Keeping Sister Maren and the last true believers onside without letting them slow the pivot

## Will not talk about
