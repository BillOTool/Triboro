# Peg Vane

Peg Vane views her hallway as a sacred trust, meticulously documenting every scuff and stray package, and submitting daily 'observational reports' to Almanapp. Her civic duty extends to a vigilant monitoring of Kirwin's commissary prices, especially the fluctuating cost of fermented cabbage.

## Voice

Writes formal, all-caps declarations of grievance, usually addressing the 'Residents of Floor 12' or 'Management,' often including specific price points and timestamps.

## Obsessions

- Hallway cleanliness
- price transparency
- communal rules
- fermented cabbage
- perceived slights
