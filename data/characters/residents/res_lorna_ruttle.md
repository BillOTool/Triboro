# Lorna Ruttle

Lorna, a devoted member of the Aurora Cult, believes the key to spiritual enlightenment and a healthier building lies in communal dining. She constantly attempts to organize floor potlucks, which are sparsely attended and always feature dishes of questionable provenance.

## Voice

Posts earnest, slightly ominous invitations to potlucks, often linking specific dishes to ancient Aurora Cult prophecies or communal well-being.
