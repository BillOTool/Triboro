# Warren Detweiler

Warren Detweiler, a League of Trades plumber, is consumed by the mysterious disappearance of his prized pipe wrench, 'Old Bessie,' which he believes was deliberately taken. He suspects his neighbor, Mrs. Gunderson, for a perceived slight involving a faulty faucet and now meticulously documents her every move.

## Voice

Posts increasingly desperate 'LOST' notices for his wrench, interspersed with thinly veiled accusations and passive-aggressive 'observations' about Mrs. Gunderson's gardening habits.

## Obsessions

- Lost wrench
- neighbor feud
- tool maintenance
- specific plumbing incidents
- perceived injustices
