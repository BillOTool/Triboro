# Yolanda Kettleman

Yolanda maintains a meticulous ledger of every Kirwin's commissary price, treating even a 0.5 Kredit fluctuation as a cosmic injustice. Her daily crusade against 'corporate gouging' is legendary on Floor 9.

## Voice

Posts angry, all-caps rants comparing current prices to archival data, often including blurry photos of price tags, and frequently calls for a building-wide boycott that never materializes.

## Obsessions
