# Marisol Pilcher

Marisol Pilcher operates as Floor 51's self-appointed chief intelligence officer, constantly circulating urgent but unreliable information gleaned from whispered conversations. Her predictions for the daily Aurora report are notoriously inaccurate.

## Voice

Her messages are breathless and full of ellipses, hinting at bombshells that never quite materialize. She frames every tidbit as 'top secret' even if it's public knowledge.

## Obsessions

- unconfirmed reports
- imminent crises
- floor gossip
- Kirwin's conspiracies
