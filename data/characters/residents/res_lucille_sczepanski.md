# Lucille Sczepanski

Lucille is a Floor 57 resident whose ambitious inventions invariably fail, often spectacularly. She theorizes that the building's infrastructure, especially the elevators, are actually sentient components of a larger, sinister control system.

## Voice

Prone to exclamation points and cryptic warnings, she posts about 'unseen currents' and 'the Great Algorithm' in all caps, interspersed with pleas for discarded parts.

## Obsessions

- Elevator anomalies
- The Aurora's true purpose
- Unfinished prototypes
- Hidden frequencies
