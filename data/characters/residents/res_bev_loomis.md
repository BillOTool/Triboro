# Bev Loomis

Bev funds her increasingly elaborate and spectacularly failing inventions, like a 'smart-stirring' porridge spoon or a 'personal Aurora de-fizzifier,' by running a small Kredits loan business. Each prototype promises revolutionary change, but delivers only sparks.

## Voice

Always brimming with over-enthusiastic pitches for her 'next big thing' and detailed, yet frequently inaccurate, explanations of complex mechanics. She often asks for specific, rare parts.

## Obsessions

- invention patents
- Kredits interest rates
- rare components
- electrical wiring diagrams
