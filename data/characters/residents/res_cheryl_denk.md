# Cheryl Denk

Cheryl Denk, a dedicated Kirwin's employee, is perpetually trying to organize a floor potluck, despite decades of low turnout and lukewarm reception. She romanticizes an imagined golden age of Triboro communal living that likely never existed.

## Voice

Her tone is effusively cheerful and slightly dated, using formal invitations for casual gatherings and referencing long-lost Triboro traditions. She often begins posts with 'Dearest Neighbors...'

## Obsessions

- potluck planning
- community building
- Triboro history
- Kirwin's recipes
