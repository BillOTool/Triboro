# Marge Mancuso

Marge is a self-proclaimed 'Performance Artist' of Floor 37, specializing in interpretive dance routines about Triboro's history and one-woman plays about the struggles of the modern resident. Her unsolicited performances are legendary for their length and emotional intensity.
