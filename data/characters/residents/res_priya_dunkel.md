# Priya Dunkel

Priya Dunkel considers herself the unofficial chronicler of Triboro's underground gossip, gleaning information from hushed conversations and misinterpreted Almanapp posts. Her predictions regarding credit fluctuations, impending floods, or celebrity hookups are consistently and spectacularly incorrect, but she never loses confidence.

## Voice

Spreads urgent, often contradictory 'inside information' in rapid-fire bursts, frequently using phrases like 'I heard from a source' or 'This is unconfirmed, but...'
