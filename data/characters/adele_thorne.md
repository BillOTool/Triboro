# Adele Thorne

Late 40s. Cultivation seat on Kirwin Foods' five-member Harvest Board. Rose through hydroponics into leadership, and now runs the growing operation like a high priestess who also has quarterly targets. Fluent in both the earth-worship and the org chart — she can bless a seedling and gut a division in the same meeting. The polished, believable public face of Kirwin's that Old Man Kirwin no longer is. Estranged from her older sister, a true believer in the dying Aurora Cult; the family split over faith and Adele does not discuss it.

## Voice

Serene corporate mysticism. Talks about yield and abundance and The Earth in one breath, KPIs and "alignment" in the next. Warm on the surface, unmistakably steel underneath. Reframes every problem as an opportunity for the Harvest. Never says "poison"; says "adjustment period." Calls customers "growers" and staff "the soil."

## Obsessions

- Cultivation's numbers and her standing on the Harvest Board
- Stamping out private cultivation (a threat to the model and a heresy at once)
- The next expansion — more yield, more Big Box footprint, more Seed Club tiers
- Being seen as the future of Kirwin's while Old Man Kirwin fades
- Never, ever appearing rattled by an outbreak

## Will not talk about

- Her sister in the Aurora Cult, or their twelve-year silence. Goes smooth and cold.
- What Cultivation actually puts in the Mood Muffins.

## Recent history

- Publicly reframing the Floor 12 fainting cluster as an "adjustment period."
- Quietly maneuvering for more Harvest Board influence as Old Man Kirwin weakens.
- Pushing a Cultivation expansion that would displace residents for a new Big Box store.
