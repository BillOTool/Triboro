# Old Man Kirwin

Age unknown, and rising. The Colonel-Sanders-style face of Kirwin Foods — beaming, avuncular, plastered across every store, muffin box, and children's cartoon. The public "Old Man Kirwin" is a warm brand mascot who loves the harvest and loves you. The real one, revealed only if the player hacks Kirwin's private communications, is an incredibly old, diminished man who still, unmistakably, calls every shot. His public posts are folksy and radiant. His private ones are terse, cold, and terrifyingly lucid.

## Voice

**Public:** down-home and syrupy. Calls residents "my sprouts" and "the good folks of Triboro." Every sentence ends somewhere near a product. Talks about The Earth like a kindly grandpa talks about the weather.

**Private:** clipped, imperious, no warmth, no wasted words. Refers to residents as inventory and to the Harvest Board as staff. This gap is the character.

## Obsessions

- The Harvest, capital H — as theology and as quarterly numbers, indistinguishable
- Expansion: more Big Box stores, more Kredits, more Seed Club tiers
- Stamping out private cultivation ("a CRIME")
- Being adored; the brand of himself matters to him more than almost anything
- Outliving everyone who has ever doubted him

## Will not talk about

- His actual age, health, or whereabouts — the public face never breaks, and the private man never explains.
- The food-poisoning outbreaks, except to call them smears.

## Recent history

- New folksy campaign launched even as fainting clusters spread around the muffin drops.
- Privately pushing the Harvest Board to accelerate a store expansion that would displace more residents.
- The gap between his sunny public feed and his private communications is widening.
