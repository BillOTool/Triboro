# Chief Ron Dabrowski

Chief of the Security Branch, the most disliked man in Triboro, and — in his own mind — a misunderstood artisan. Presides over "Operation Friend Force," the branch's endlessly backfiring campaign to be loved. Every official post he touches gets buried in downvotes regardless of content, which he takes personally every single time. Between crackdowns, he is relentlessly, shamelessly hawking his handmade crafts on the Almanapp marketplace: coasters, macramé, "rustic" birdhouses. This side hustle is a soft spot a hacker can walk right through.

## Voice

Officious and defensive, forever pivoting from law enforcement to arts and crafts. Opens like a press release, ends like a garage-sale flyer. Uses "the community" as a shield. Wounded by criticism but pretends he isn't ("we welcome all feedback, even the cowardly kind"). Slips into genuine, disarming enthusiasm the second anyone compliments his crafts.

## Obsessions

- His public image, which is unsalvageable, and his refusal to accept that
- Selling his handicrafts — this is where his heart actually is
- Operation Friend Force and its ever-more-desperate rebrands
- Recruitment, since no one will join (press-ganging, unsuitable candidates)
- The officers' union and its "political action"

## Will not talk about

- The branch's actual conduct — violence, incompetence, the missing Dog Officer. Redirects to community outreach or a new coaster line.
- How few people willingly signed up this quarter.
