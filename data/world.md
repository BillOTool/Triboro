# Triboro

A massive apartment complex sealed off from the outside world for 73 years by toxic smog called **The Aurora** (currently 847 PPM). Nobody alive has ever been outside. The Outside is mythology. The building is the entire world.

## Factions

- **Kirwin Foods** — corporate monopoly that controls the food supply. Three generations of Kirwin family rule. Pushing for more power.
- **The League of Trades** — blue-collar union of plumbers, electricians, mechanics. Inherited memberships. Actually keeps the building running. Informal leader: Gus Pelletier.
- **The Security Branch** — building police. Mostly incompetent bureaucrats.
- **The Aurora Cult** — declining religious group. Believes the smog is a spiritual trial. Was much bigger a generation ago.
- **The Triboro Council** — weak elected government. An election is coming.

## Geography

- **Theater District** — Floors 33-36. Real cultural life.
- **Floor 61** — rumored sealed, "doesn't exist."
- **Floors 30-45** — water rationing crisis, original infrastructure failing.

## Media

- **Babbage Family Press** — main news outlet, run by the Babbage family since Year 5.
- **Community Newsletter** — grassroots alternative.
- **Almanapp** — Triboro's social network / messaging platform. Where the feed lives.

## Economy

Currency is **credits**. Wildly volatile — hyperinflation/deflation swings. 200 credits could be a fortune or worthless by the time you finish a job. NPCs acknowledge this casually. The exchange rate ticks constantly.

## Tone

Funny, dry, character-driven. Onion-style. Weirdness treated as ordinary. Mundane treated as cosmic. Three generations of isolation have produced strange customs, dead slang, lost knowledge, and beautiful obsessions.
